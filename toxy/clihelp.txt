Description.
Usage: ./Toxy [--version | -v] [--help | -h] [--game | -g <name>] [-puzzle-size | -s <value> | <value,value>]

LargeDescription.

Startup:
         -v, --version		display the version and exit.
         -h, --help		print this help.
         -g, --game		type of the game independently of case, default=LoopOver .
         -s, -puzzle-size	puzzle size, default=[9,9].

Examples:
        ./Toxy -g slic -s [10,10] | 10,10 | (10,10)
